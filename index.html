The WeeCare Sensor

A compact, clip-on moisture sensor with a thin capacitive strip that turns any incontinence pad into a precision diagnostic tool — automatically measuring fluid volume and sending data to a smartphone app.

Explore the Design View App Mockups
Two Components
Simple, Elegant Design
A reusable sensor pod and a disposable sensing strip. Snap together, clip to any pad, and go.

Sensor Pod

Compact enclosed module with microcontroller, Bluetooth radio, and rechargeable battery. Clips onto the pad edge or waistband. Reusable across pad changes.

+

Sensing Strip

Thin, flexible strip with parallel capacitive electrodes. Adheres inside any incontinence pad. Disposable — replace with each pad change.

Data Flow
From Pad to Insight
Fully automated. No patient input needed for sensing — data flows from strip to pod to phone to clinician.

Moisture Contact

Fluid reaches the strip's copper electrodes, changing capacitance.

Pod Reads Signal

Microcontroller inside the pod reads capacitance and estimates volume.

BLE to Phone

Data transmits wirelessly via Bluetooth to the WeeCare app.

Automated Diary

Complete bladder diary with volumes, timestamps, and patterns for clinicians.

Setup
Ready in Seconds
Designed for caregivers and clinical staff. No technical knowledge needed.
1

Stick Strip to Pad

Peel the adhesive backing and press the thin sensing strip inside any incontinence pad.

2

Snap On the Pod

Click the reusable sensor pod onto the strip's connector tab. It clips securely to the pad edge.

3

Auto-Pairs with App

Open the WeeCare app — the pod connects via Bluetooth automatically. Monitoring begins instantly.

Capabilities
Designed for Real Life
Every detail shaped by feedback from patients, caregivers, and clinicians.

Works with Any Pad

Universal strip fits any standard incontinence pad or underwear. Auto-calibrates via pre-built signal library.

50 mL Precision

Capacitive sensing electrodes measure fluid volume with clinical-grade accuracy for bladder diary standards.

Real-Time Alerts

BLE streams data continuously. Instant push notification when moisture threshold is exceeded.

Moisture-Proof

Sealed strip and enclosed pod designed for continuous use in wet conditions without degradation.

Comfortable & Discreet

Thin strip conforms to pad. Compact pod clips discreetly. Designed for all-day, all-night wear.

Reusable Pod

Pod is rechargeable and reusable. Only the low-cost sensing strip is disposable, replaced with each pad change.
